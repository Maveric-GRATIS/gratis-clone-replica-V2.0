3. Partner NGO Vetting Process
We don't donate to just anyone. Every NGO partner must meet strict verification standards and maintain ongoing transparency.
3.1 Selection Criteria
• Verified Registration: Must be registered NGO/non-profit with government
• Financial Transparency: Public financial statements and annual reports required
• Proven Impact: Documented track record of measurable outcomes
• Ethical Operations: No corruption, exploitation, or appropriation
• Alignment: Mission aligned with GRATIS focus areas (water, arts, education)
